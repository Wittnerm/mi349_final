Top-Quality Service at Affordable Prices

Based out of Port Huron, MI, Roadside Truck & Fleet Repairs is a purely mobile heavy truck and trailer repair company that provides on-site service to the St. Clair County area. Roadside is a one-man operation, headed by Rick Wittner, who has over 25 years of professional experience in diesel and automotive mechanics. Rick operates out of his service van, which is eqipped with all of the necessary tools and equipment to get the job done right. Roadside operates 24/7, 365 days a year for emergency services.

Services We Offer

Roadside offers a wide range of services for medium and heavy commercial trucks, trailers, and mobile fleet operations including but not limited to:

  • Battery
  • Brakes
  • Preventative maintainence
  • Check engine light diagnostics
  • Electrical
  • Oil change
  • Steering/Suspention
  • Transmission

  • Air conditioning
